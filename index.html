What LISBITE is about

LISBITE makes indie games that feel dangerous, cinematic, and reactive. The goal is to build games where players can create their own stories through systems rather than only following scripted moments.

The studio is currently in its early stage, focused on prototypes, visual direction, and building a strong first release.
