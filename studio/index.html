Studio

Small team. Strange worlds.

LISBITE is an early indie game studio focused on atmospheric games with strong systems, dangerous worlds, and memorable player-made moments.

The current focus is building a natural disaster survival prototype, improving technical skills, and shaping a clear visual identity for future releases.

Mood

Theatrical dark fantasy

Rich purples, magenta highlights, star maps, heavy silhouettes, and expressive lighting.

Scale

Ruined distance

Lonely towers, ancient structures, mountains, and spaces that feel bigger than the player.

Space

Industrial pressure

Vertical structures, metal, darkness, pipes, platforms, shafts, and collapsing environments.
